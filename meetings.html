Meetings & Lectures

Meeting Schedule

MELCOM UK holds twice-yearly meetings:

  • Winter Meeting: December/January
  • Summer Meeting: July

Meetings are held at various academic institutions across the UK and provide opportunities for networking, professional development, and scholarly exchange.

Pearson Memorial Lecture

Our annual Pearson Memorial Lecture is a prestigious event featuring distinguished speakers from the field of Middle Eastern studies and librarianship.

Recent Pearson Lectures

  • 2017: Jubilee panel celebrating 50 years of MELCOM (Cambridge)
  • 2012: "Books as history in medieval Iran" by Professor Charles Melville (LSE)
  • 2011: "The novelist as political guide: Sun' Allah Ibrahim and the Egyptian revolution" by Professor Paul Starkey (University of Exeter)

Meeting Venues

Our meetings have been hosted by prestigious institutions including:

  • University of Cambridge
  • London School of Economics (LSE)
  • University of Exeter
  • British Library, London
  • SOAS University of London
