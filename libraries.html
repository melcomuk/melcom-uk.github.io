Libraries & Collections

Partnership with BRISMES

Since the establishment of the British Society for Middle Eastern Studies (BRISMES) in 1973, MELCOM has maintained close relations with it, seeking to represent the interests and needs of libraries within the field of Middle Eastern studies in Britain, and to enhance its members' awareness of library and bibliographical resources in their field.

MELCOM has provided a special section in the Society's Bulletin (now the British Journal of Middle Eastern Studies) for reviews of bibliographies and other reference works and occasional articles of a bibliographical nature.

International Cooperation

Since 1979, MELCOM has co-operated with other Middle East librarians in Europe – especially France, Germany, Netherlands and Spain – by holding joint international conferences to discuss matters of common interest, exchange information and to hear scholarly papers on:

  • Historical bibliography
  • Codicology
  • Library history
  • Orientalism
  • Related scholarly topics

MELCOM International

At the 1987 Conference in Tunis, a new organisation was constituted, called MELCOM International: the European Association of Middle East Librarians. This organisation has taken over the responsibility for organising international conferences, as well as extending Middle East library co-operation in new directions.
