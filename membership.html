Membership & Officers

Membership

Membership and participation in MELCOM (UK) are open without formality to all who are interested in the committee's aims and activities.

Who Should Join?

The following are especially encouraged to join:

  • Librarians
  • Information officers
  • Archivists
  • Bibliographers
  • Codicologists
  • Academics, scholars and students of the Middle East
  • Information scientists
  • Booksellers
  • Bibliophiles
  • Students of Middle Eastern librarianship
  • Academics and scholars with a particular interest in libraries and/or bibliography

Membership Benefits

  • No membership fee
  • Twice-yearly meetings (December/January and July)
  • Access to the Pearson Memorial Lecture series
  • Networking opportunities with Middle East library professionals
  • Professional development opportunities

To join: Contact the Secretary for further details.
