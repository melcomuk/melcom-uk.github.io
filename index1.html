About MELCOM UK

MELCOM UK is the British branch of the Middle East Libraries Committee, founded in 1967. We are dedicated to promoting cooperation among librarians and information professionals working with Middle Eastern materials.

Our Mission

  • Promote cooperation among Middle East librarians
  • Facilitate networking and professional development
  • Organize meetings and lectures
  • Maintain relationships with international library organizations
  • Support research and scholarship in Middle Eastern studies

Key Activities

We organize twice-yearly meetings (December/January and July) featuring the prestigious Pearson Memorial Lecture series. Our events provide opportunities for networking, professional development, and scholarly exchange among members of the Middle Eastern library community.
